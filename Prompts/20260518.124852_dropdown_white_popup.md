# Implementation Plan for Bright White Dropdown Popup Background

**User Prompt:**
> the inside of drop down is very dark. make it bright and normal color

1. **Investigate the Problem**:
    * Currently, when the custom `AppCompatSpinner` expands, its dropdown popup window inherits colors from default dark style resource overlay classes on certain devices/configurations, causing it to display a very dark background.
    * This dark background severely reduces text contrast against our high-contrast slate text `#1A1A2E`.
    * Setting the popup background explicitly to a crisp, bright, pure white color (`#FFFFFF`) guarantees ideal legibility and a normal, professional look across all devices.

2. **Implementation Steps**:
    * **`activity_settings.xml`**: Add `android:popupBackground="#FFFFFF"` directly onto `spFrequency` to force a bright, pristine white dropdown background card.
