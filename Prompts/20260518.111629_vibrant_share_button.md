# Implementation Plan for Vibrant Share Button (High Contrast Dialog Option)

**User Prompt:**
> DELETE button color is OK. the SHARE button is still not clearly visible with the grey background of the alertbox.

1. **Investigate the Problem**:
    * Currently, the positive "Share" dialog button text color is set to `#0F3460`.
    * Because `#0F3460` is a very deep dark blue, it blends into the grey background of standard Android AlertDialog dialogs, causing low visibility and poor contrast.
    * The "Delete" button (set to a vivid red `#D32F2F`) is highly visible and approved by the user.
    * To achieve matching high contrast for the "Share" button, we will transition it to **`#1E88E5`** (Vibrant Material Blue), which is a bright, energetic, and highly readable accent blue specifically optimized by Material Design guidelines for grey and dark backgrounds.

2. **Implementation Steps**:
    * **`TracksListActivity.java`**: Update the dialog positive button color in `onShareClick()` from `#0F3460` to `#1E88E5`.
    * **`MapActivity.java`**: Update the dialog positive button color in `shareTrack()` from `#0F3460` to `#1E88E5`.
